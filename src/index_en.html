About 8 Meters

Who We Are

The building where 8 Meters resides has been our family’s safe haven for more than sixty years. As Taipei evolves into a city that embraces the notion of modernity and innovation, old houses are on the brink of extinction under the trend of urban renewal. We want to do our best to preserve the old house and bestow new meaning to the building by integrating modern elements into the original structure.

How We Work

A space for working, relaxing, chatting, exchanging information and expertise as well as making friends. We welcome people of all ages and experiences and hope that you'll weave your new experiences into the texture of the old house.
